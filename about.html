About Nuevo Puerta Real Estate

Founded in 2019 by Rosibelle G. Ituralde, Nuevo Puerta Real Estate began with a simple yet powerful vision — to provide accessible and high-quality real estate opportunities for individuals and businesses. What started with the sale of small residential lots in Pasonanca has grown into a trusted name in the real estate industry, offering a wide range of residential and commercial lots in prime locations.

At Nuevo Puerta, we believe that owning land is more than just a transaction — it’s a step toward building dreams, securing investments, and creating lasting legacies. Our lots are strategically located to cater to both aspiring homeowners and entrepreneurs, giving them the freedom to design and develop spaces that suit their visions.

Guided by integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction, Nuevo Puerta Real Estate is committed to helping clients find the perfect property — whether it’s for building a dream home, starting a business, or making a solid investment.

Join us in opening doors to endless possibilities.
