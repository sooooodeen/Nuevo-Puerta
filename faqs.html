Real Estate FAQs

What is real estate? ▼
Real estate refers to land and any permanent structures attached to it, such as houses or buildings.
What are the different types of real estate? ▼
Real estate includes residential, commercial, industrial, and land properties.
What should I consider before buying a property? ▼
Consider location, market trends, financing options, property condition, and legal aspects before purchasing.
How do I get a mortgage for a home purchase? ▼
You need to check your credit score, research lenders, get pre-approved, and submit required documents.
What is the difference between a real estate agent and a broker? ▼
Agents help clients buy or sell properties, while brokers have additional qualifications to manage agents.
How can I increase my home’s value before selling? ▼
Upgrade kitchens and bathrooms, enhance curb appeal, and repair structural issues.
What is a real estate appraisal? ▼
An appraisal is a professional evaluation of a property's value based on market trends and conditions.
What are closing costs in a real estate transaction? ▼
Closing costs include loan fees, title insurance, legal fees, and taxes, typically 2-5% of the property price.
Is real estate a good investment? ▼
Yes, real estate investments can appreciate over time and generate rental income.
What are common mistakes to avoid when buying real estate? ▼
Avoid skipping inspections, ignoring hidden costs, overextending your budget, and rushing decisions.
